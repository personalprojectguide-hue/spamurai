Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

What we collect

When you sign in with Google, we receive your name, email address, and profile picture from Google. We store this to identify your account.

When you scan your inbox, we read the From: headers of your emails to identify senders. We do not read, store, or transmit the content of any emails.

What we don't do

We do not sell, share, or monetise your data in any way. We do not store your emails. We do not track you across other websites.

Google API

Spamurai uses the Gmail API to read sender information and perform deletions on your behalf. We only request the permissions necessary to provide this service. Your Gmail credentials are never stored on our servers.

Data storage

We store your name, email, and profile picture in a local database to track account activity. This data is not shared with third parties.

Deleting your data

You can revoke Spamurai's access to your Gmail at any time via Google Account Permissions. To delete your account data, email us.

Contact

For any privacy questions, contact us at the email associated with this app.
